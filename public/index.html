Terms of Service

LiftLab provides general fitness information only. Consult a qualified professional before starting any new program. You train at your own risk.

2. Accuracy of estimates

1RM calculations use a weighted average of Brzycki, Lander, Epley, and Mayhew formulas. These are estimates — never attempt a true 1RM without a spotter.

3. Your responsibility

You are solely responsible for how you use this information. LiftLab is not liable for any injury or damage from following generated advice.

Account data is stored securely on our servers. Browser-only data stays in localStorage. We do not sell any data.

Do not abuse or spam the service. We reserve the right to block access for misuse.
